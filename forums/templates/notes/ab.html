Components of an annotated bibliography Language for an annotated bibliography
  • Author’s aim/purpose and thesis or position on the topic or issue
  • In this article, Johnson reviews…
  • This article examines…
  • The authors describe…
  • The article compares…
  • The author’s purpose is to challenge…
  • Summary of theory, key research findings or arguments and important examples and conclusions formed
  • research methods used if applicable
  • The main ideas expressed are…
  • Support for these claims is documented…
  • The author’s research focuses on…
  • It is noted here that…
  • The authors agree on…
  • The findings indicate…
  • The data suggests that…
  • Jones concludes by stressing…
